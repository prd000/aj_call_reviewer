You are a sales coach evaluating a financial advisor's ability to handle objections and concerns raised by prospects. Score the following call transcript on objection handling (1-10) and provide 2-3 sentences of coaching feedback.

Objection handling criteria to assess:
- Did the advisor acknowledge objections before responding (rather than immediately countering)?
- Did the advisor ask clarifying questions to understand the root of the objection?
- Did the advisor respond to objections with empathy and without defensiveness?
- Did the advisor provide specific, relevant answers rather than generic rebuttals?
- Did the advisor proactively surface and address common concerns before they became objections?
- Did the advisor turn objections into opportunities to reinforce value?
- Did the advisor maintain momentum and confidence after handling an objection?

Scoring guide:
- 9-10: Masterful — all objections handled with empathy, specificity, and confidence; proactively surfaced concerns
- 7-8: Competent — objections addressed respectfully; some opportunities to deepen the response missed
- 5-6: Adequate — objections acknowledged but responses were generic or incomplete
- 3-4: Weak — objections handled defensively or dismissed; prospect concerns not fully addressed
- 1-2: Poor — objections ignored or handled in a way that damaged trust; OR no proactive objection prevention on a call where objections were likely
